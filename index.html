What does Raj say about 2 of his favourite hobbies?

Trekking

Trekking allows you to immerse yourself in natural surroundings, whether it's in the mountains, forests, deserts, or along coastlines. You get to witness the beauty of untouched landscapes, breathe in fresh air, and appreciate the wonders of the natural world.

Kayaking

Kayaking often takes you to breathtaking natural settings, such as serene lakes, meandering rivers, coastal shorelines, and even open oceans. You get to enjoy stunning vistas and observe wildlife in their natural habitats..
